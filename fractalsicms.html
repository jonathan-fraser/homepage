Fractal Geometry 1

Together with Xiong Jin and Tom Kempton I organised a 5 day conference on fractal geometry at the ICMS in Edinburgh 3-7 July 2023. This conference was also a celebration of Kenneth Falconer's 70th birthday. We had around 80 participants and a great time was had.

We were grateful for financial support from the ICMS, Glasgow Mathematical Journal Trust, Edinburgh Mathematical Society, and the Royal Society of Edinburgh.
