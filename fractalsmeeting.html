Fractal geometry and dimension theory

I hosted a one day meeting on fractal geometry and dimension theory in the University of Manchester School of Mathematics on Wednesday the 22nd of July 2015. This meeting was partially funded by an LMS grant (Scheme 1: Celebrating New Appointments) and partially funded by the University of Manchester School of Mathematics (MIMS).
The schedule was as follows:
    • 12.30pm - 1.30pm: Lunch in the Business School
    • 1.30pm - 2.30pm: Jonathan Fraser: Fourier analytic properties of the Brownian graph. Abstract
    • 2.30pm-3.30pm: Mark Pollicott: Mobility of stationary measures. Slides, Abstract
    • 3.30pm - 4pm: Coffee break.
    • 4pm - 5pm: Kenneth Falconer: Self-similar Sets: Projections, Sections and Percolation. Slides, Abstract
    • 5.00pm - 7pm: Drinks reception.
    • 7pm: Dinner in EastZeast.
