Dynamical Systems and Analysis Seminar

The Dynamical Systems and Analysis Seminar runs every week during the semester at 3pm on Tuesday in Frank Adams 1. It would be great to see lots of people there, so if you're at all interested then feel free to come along! There will often be plans for lunch and/or dinner and drinks with the speaker. If anyone wants more information, they should feel free to contact me: jon.fraser32@gmail.com.

For previous schedules, click here.
